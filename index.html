THE PROBLEM

Biological Research
Is Broken by Silos

Researchers today spend 60–70% of their time managing disconnected tools, reformatting data between platforms, and waiting on compute that lives outside their institution. The science is advancing — the infrastructure isn't.

VigyanLLM was built to collapse the entire research workflow into a single, sovereign, intelligent system.

Siloed, Single-Purpose Tools

Today's biotech AI is fragmented. One tool for protein folding. Another for docking. Another for PCR. Researchers stitch them together manually, losing hours and introducing error at every handoff.

Data Sovereignty & Privacy Risk

Sending sensitive genomic, clinical, and patient data to third-party cloud APIs violates data sovereignty principles. Healthcare institutions cannot afford this exposure — yet most current AI tools demand it.

Cost Inaccessible to Most Research Institutions

Enterprise-grade platforms like Schrödinger cost tens of thousands of dollars annually. The majority of research labs — especially in emerging markets — simply cannot access state-of-the-art computational biology tools.

No System Covers the Full Research Lifecycle

From hypothesis to validated protocol, a researcher touches six or more disconnected systems. No existing platform automates the complete pipeline — primer design, structure prediction, docking, expression analysis, and clinical reasoning — under one sovereign roof.

MULTI-AGENT ARCHITECTURE

Three Agents.
One Research Pipeline.

VigyanLLM does not rely on a single monolithic model. Three specialized AI agents operate in sequence — each with a distinct role — ensuring that every research output is orchestrated, reasoned, and verified before it reaches the researcher.

AGENT 01 · ORCHESTRATION

Core

Orchestration Layer

Parses research intent, decomposes complex multi-step biological queries, and routes sub-tasks to the appropriate specialized agents. Core synthesizes all intermediate outputs into a coherent, structured final report — acting as the primary interface between the researcher and the rest of the AI system.

Prompt RoutingTask DecompositionReport Synthesis
AGENT 02 · DOMAIN REASONING

SubBrain

Domain Reasoning Engine

The deep-domain specialist. SubBrain handles biological knowledge retrieval, sequence analysis, PCR thermodynamics, protein structure prediction, RNA-Seq interpretation, and KEGG/Reactome pathway mapping. Fine-tuned across 46 specialized training batches on 320,000+ biomedical and genomic records.

PCR LogicRNA-SeqAlphaFold IntegrationPathway Analysis
AGENT 03 · VERIFICATION

ChinhAI

Verification & Quality Gate

The validation layer that ensures research-grade accuracy at every step. ChinhAI cross-checks primer designs against off-target binding databases, validates molecular docking results, flags sgRNA-DNA Heteroduplex Instability in CRISPR workflows, and enforces full protocol integrity before any output reaches the researcher.
