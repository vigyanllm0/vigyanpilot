  VigyanLLM — Hardened DNS Email Security Records
  Domain: vigyanllm.in
  Provider: Hostinger DNS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Apply these DNS records at your domain registrar/DNS provider
(Hostinger hPanel → Domains → vigyanllm.in → DNS Zone Editor).

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1. SPF RECORD (TXT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Host/Name: @
  Type:       TXT
  Value:      v=spf1 mx include:_spf.hostinger.com -all

  ━━━ Hardening Notes ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  • HARD FAIL (-all) — rejects all mail from unauthorised senders
    (replaces the prior softfail ~all that allowed spoofing).
  • include:_spf.hostinger.com — authorises Hostinger's mail
    relay infrastructure to send on your behalf.
  • mx — authorises any server listed in the domain's MX records.
  • No third-party ESP includes (Mailgun, SendGrid, etc.) unless
    explicitly needed. Add only as required.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  2. DKIM RECORD (TXT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Host/Name: hostinger._domainkey
  Type:       TXT
  Value:      v=DKIM1; h=sha256; k=rsa; p=MIGfMA0GCSqGSIb3DQEBAQUAA4GNADCBiQKBgQC1...

  ━━━ Setup Instructions (Hostinger) ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  1. Log in to Hostinger hPanel → Emails → Email Mail Services
  2. Click "Manage" on your vigyanllm.in email plan
  3. Go to "Authentication" → "DKIM"
  4. Enable DKIM signing
  5. The public key will be displayed — copy it as-is
  6. Create a TXT record in DNS Zone:
       Host/Name: hostinger._domainkey
       Value:     (paste the full DKIM public key string)
  7. DKIM alignment: Use "relaxed" alignment mode (Hostinger default)
     This means the d=domain in the DKIM signature must be a
     subdomain of the From: domain (vigyanllm.in).

  ━━━ Verification ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  Test with: dig TXT hostinger._domainkey.vigyanllm.in

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  3. DMARC RECORD (TXT)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Host/Name: _dmarc
  Type:       TXT
  Value:      v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; sp=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in; ruf=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in; pct=100; fo=1

  ━━━ DMARC Policy Interpretation ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  • v=DMARC1                          — DMARC version
  • p=quarantine                      — Policy for the organisational domain:
                                        mail failing SPF+DKIM should go to
                                        spam/quarantine (upgrade to p=reject
                                        after 30 days of monitoring).
  • sp=quarantine                     — Same policy for subdomains
  • rua=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in    — Aggregate (daily) reports sent here
  • ruf=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in    — Forensic (per-failure) reports
  • pct=100                           — Policy applies to 100% of mail
  • fo=1                              — Generate forensic reports on any
                                        authentication failure

  ━━━ Rollout Strategy ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  Phase 1 (Monitor — 7 days):
    v=DMARC1; p=none; rua=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in; fo=1
    → Observe report data without affecting delivery.

  Phase 2 (Quarantine — 14 days): ← CURRENT RECOMMENDATION
    v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; sp=quarantine; rua=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in; ...

  Phase 3 (Reject):
    v=DMARC1; p=reject; sp=reject; rua=mailto:dmarc@vigyanllm.in; ...

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  5. APPLYING RECORDS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  In Hostinger hPanel:
    1. Go to "Domains" → click vigyanllm.in → "DNS Zone Editor"
    2. Delete any existing SPF records (keep only the one above)
    3. Add the TXT records one by one as specified
    4. Wait 5–30 minutes for DNS propagation
    5. Verify with:
       dig TXT vigyanllm.in         # SPF
       dig TXT hostinger._domainkey.vigyanllm.in  # DKIM
       dig TXT _dmarc.vigyanllm.in  # DMARC

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  EMAIL SPOOFING — RISK MITIGATION SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Before fix: SPF = ~all (softfail), DKIM = absent, DMARC = absent
              → Grade F. Anyone could spoof @vigyanllm.in.
  After fix:  SPF = -all (hardfail), DKIM = signing enabled,
              DMARC = p=quarantine (→ p=reject)
              → Grade A. Spoofed emails are rejected/quarantined.

  Alignment check structure:
  - SPF alignment: Return-Path domain must match From domain
    (Hostinger sets Return-Path automatically)
  - DKIM alignment: d=domain in signature must match From domain
    (relaxed mode allows subdomain match)
  - DMARC requires at least one of SPF or DKIM to align
