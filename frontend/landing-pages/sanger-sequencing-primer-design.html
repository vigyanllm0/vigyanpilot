Frequently Asked Questions: Sanger sequencing primer design

How do I design primers for Sanger sequencing?

Sanger sequencing primers need to anneal 50-150 bp upstream of your region of interest, have a Tm of 52-58°C for optimal reaction conditions, and avoid secondary structures that interfere with clean extension. VigyanLLM designs sequencing primers that satisfy all these requirements while also passing the full 24-step validation pipeline for specificity and stability.
