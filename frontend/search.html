Frequently Asked Questions About Sequence Search Tool

Everything you need to know about using this tool

Is the Sequence Search tool free?

The sequence search tool offers 5 free searches per user. Searches use backend databases and alignment compute resources. After 5 free uses, a subscription is required.

What can I search for?

You can search for DNA, RNA, or protein sequences against NCBI databases, custom reference genomes, or uploaded sequence sets.

How is searching different from BLAST?

Sequence search is optimized for quick exact and near-exact matches across large databases. BLAST is more sensitive for distant homology but slower. Both complement each other.

Do I need an account?

No, you can search without an account for your first 5 searches. Creating an account enables subscription management.

How do I subscribe?

Subscribe through VPrime 2.0 starting at ₹99/day. Monthly plans from ₹2,499/month provide unlimited searches for all members.
