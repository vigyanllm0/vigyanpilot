Sequencing Technologies: NGS, Sanger, and Platform-Specific Primer Design

DNA sequencing technologies have evolved dramatically, from traditional Sanger sequencing to high-throughput next-generation sequencing (NGS) and long-read single-molecule sequencing. Each platform has unique primer design requirements: Sanger primers need optimal positioning for read coverage, Illumina NGS library primers require adapter-compatible overhangs, and Oxford Nanopore accommodates longer amplicons with higher GC tolerance. VigyanLLM designs platform-specific primers for all major sequencing technologies and provides comprehensive downstream analysis tools.
