PCR Analysis Workflows Supported

Conventional PCR

Design primers for standard end-point PCR with amplicon sizing 60-2000 bp. Includes Tm optimisation, GC content balancing, and specificity validation.

  • Amplicon length 60-2000 bp
  • Tm 55-65°C with ±1°C uniformity
  • GC content 40-60%
  • 3' end stability check

Quantitative PCR (qPCR)

SYBR Green and TaqMan assay design with exon junction spanning, amplicon sizing 60-150 bp, and probe Tm differential optimisation.

  • 60-150 bp amplicon
  • Exon-exon junction design
  • TaqMan probe Tm 5-10°C above primers
  • SYBR Green melt curve prediction

Multiplex PCR

Design 2-6 plex assays with amplicon size differentiation, Tm harmonisation, and cross-dimer elimination for simultaneous target detection.

  • 2 to 6 target multiplexing
  • Amplicon size separation ≥20 bp
  • Tm harmonisation ±1°C
  • Cross-dimer matrix validation

Long-Range PCR

Design primers for 5-50 kb amplicons optimised for Nanopore and PacBio sequencing platforms with specialised polymerase compatibility.

  • 5-50 kb amplicons
  • Nanopore/PacBio optimisation
  • Polymerase-specific design
  • Amplicon tiling strategies
