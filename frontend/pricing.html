Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VigyanLLM pricing and subscriptions.

Can I switch between plans? ▼
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes take effect immediately. If you downgrade, the new limits apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
What happens when I hit my monthly limit? ▼
You can wait for your quota to reset at the next billing cycle, or purchase a Daily Pass to unlock additional primer designs and docking runs immediately. Unused quota does not roll over.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? ▼
The Academic Free tier is always available at no cost. For paid plans, we offer institute-wide evaluations for academic labs and a 14-day trial for corporate R&D teams. Contact us at contact@vigyanllm.in to set up an evaluation.
Do you offer educational or non-profit discounts? ▼
Yes. Academic institutions with valid .ac.in, .edu, or .res.in domains qualify for our Academic Free tier. For lab-wide or institution-wide subscriptions, the Lab / Academic Institute plan offers the best value. Contact us for custom pricing for non-profit research organizations.
Still have questions? Reach out to contact@vigyanllm.in — we typically respond within 4 hours.
