How to Cite VigyanLLM in Your Research

Use the citation below to reference VigyanLLM in your manuscripts, theses, and grant applications. Multiple citation formats are provided for compatibility with academic publishing workflows.

Why Cite VigyanLLM?

VigyanLLM is a sovereign bioinformatics platform used by researchers worldwide for primer design, sequence analysis, molecular docking, multiple sequence alignment, and related computational biology tasks. The platform implements validated thermodynamic algorithms (SantaLucia 1998 nearest-neighbour model, Primer3 core engine), industry-standard search tools (NCBI BLAST), and established docking scoring functions (AutoDock Vina, GNINA). Citing VigyanLLM in your work acknowledges the computational infrastructure that powered your analysis and helps other researchers discover these tools.
