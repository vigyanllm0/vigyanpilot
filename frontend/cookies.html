Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-30

VigyanLLM is a sovereign biomedical AI platform. We minimise data collection and do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising or analytics personalisation. This policy explains the storage mechanisms used by our platform and your choices.

What We Use and Why

The VigyanLLM Primer tool uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage for essential application functions. We do not set persistent third-party cookies for tracking.

pf_tokensessionStorageAuthentication session token — cleared on logout or tab close

Third-Party Services

The following third-party services may set their own cookies or storage when you interact with their components:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 — when you sign in with Google, Google sets authentication cookies governed by Google's privacy policy
  • Razorpay — when you complete a payment, Razorpay's hosted checkout may set session cookies necessary for payment processing
  • Google Fonts — our pages load typefaces from Google Fonts; Google may log requests per its privacy policy

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective service's privacy policy for details.

Your Choices

You can clear localStorage and sessionStorage at any time through your browser settings (typically Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Site Data). Clearing this data will sign you out and remove your locally stored design history but will not affect your account on our servers.

Most browsers also provide private or incognito modes that clear sessionStorage automatically when you close the window.

Do Not Track

Our platform does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals because we do not engage in cross-site tracking. All data stored locally is associated only with your session on this domain.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
