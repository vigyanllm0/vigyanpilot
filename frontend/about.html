About VigyanLLM: Sovereign Biomedical AI for Research

VigyanLLM is a sovereign biomedical AI platform headquartered in India, providing validated primer and probe design, PCR assay development, and bioinformatics tools for research laboratories, biotech companies, and pharmaceutical institutions worldwide. Built from the ground up with a 22-step validated pipeline, VigyanLLM ensures every primer pair meets rigorous biophysical, thermodynamic, and specificity standards before it reaches the laboratory bench.

Our Mission

VigyanLLM was founded with a singular mission: to democratize validated primer design and make advanced bioinformatics accessible to every research laboratory, regardless of geography, budget, or institutional size. For decades, computational molecular biology tools have been concentrated in well-funded institutions in developed nations, leaving a vast global research community reliant on fragmented, outdated, or prohibitively expensive software. We believe that the ability to design high-quality primers and validate assays computationally should not be a privilege — it should be a standard research utility available to every scientist on the planet.

Our mission extends beyond accessibility. We aim to build the most comprehensive, sovereign, and trustworthy biomedical AI platform in existence — one that operates entirely on-premises, never sends sensitive genomic data to external cloud services, and produces audit-ready outputs that meet the documentation requirements of regulated research environments. We are committed to India-first pricing that reflects the economic realities of research funding in the Global South, while maintaining global-quality validation standards that satisfy peer review and regulatory scrutiny.

We envision a future where every molecular biology experiment begins with computationally validated reagents, where primer-dimer failures and off-target amplification are caught before they waste precious lab resources, and where researchers spend their time on discovery rather than on troubleshooting poorly designed oligonucleotides.

Our Technology

VigyanLLM's core technology is a proprietary 22-step validated primer and probe design pipeline that combines Primer3 candidate generation with advanced biophysical, thermodynamic, and biological validation. Unlike conventional primer design tools that stop at melting temperature and GC content, VigyanLLM performs a comprehensive multi-stage assessment that includes SantaLucia 1998 nearest-neighbour thermodynamic modelling with salt and magnesium correction (Owczarzy 2004, von Ahsen 2001), BLAST-based specificity screening against NCBI reference genomes and transcriptomes, hairpin and self-dimer free energy calculations with strict delta-G thresholds, cross-dimer and heterodimer interaction analysis for multiplex compatibility, dbSNP variant filtering to exclude primers overlapping known polymorphisms, repeat element masking using Dfam and local complexity algorithms, amplicon secondary structure prediction at extension temperature, and manufacturing quality checks including GC clamp verification and synthesis failure mode analysis.

The platform is built on a multi-agent AI architecture comprising three coordinated agents: Core, which decomposes research queries and orchestrates the pipeline; SubBrain, which handles domain-specific biological reasoning and sequence analysis; and ChinhAI, a dedicated verification agent that cross-checks every output against off-target databases and physical benchmarks before releasing results to the researcher. This three-agent architecture provides a structural defence against AI hallucination — a critical requirement in biomedical applications where incorrect outputs can corrupt experiments, waste weeks of laboratory work, or compromise clinical decisions.

All inference is powered by the VigyanInferenceEngine, a native GGUF inference engine that runs entirely on local or dedicated hardware with no external API dependencies. The platform has been trained on 46 specialized batches comprising over 320,000 purpose-built biomedical records, each targeting a precise biological subdomain from clinical reasoning to CRISPR structural anomaly detection. The pipeline generates comprehensive PDF audit reports that document the pass/fail status of every validation step, providing full traceability for regulated laboratory workflows and publication support.

Who We Serve

VigyanLLM serves the full spectrum of molecular biology researchers and institutions across academic, clinical, industrial, and forensic domains. Academic research laboratories and universities use VigyanLLM for gene expression analysis, RT-PCR primer design, and SNP genotyping assays, benefiting from India-first pricing that makes advanced computational validation accessible to student researchers and principal investigators alike. Biotech and pharmaceutical R&D teams integrate VigyanLLM into their drug discovery workflows for target identification, biomarker validation, and quality control of therapeutic products, relying on the platform's batch processing and audit-ready reporting for regulatory submissions.

Clinical diagnostic laboratories leverage VigyanLLM's Research Use Only pipeline for pathogen detection assay development, infectious disease surveillance, and oncology biomarker screening, with the platform's strict validation thresholds ensuring robust and reproducible results. Forensic DNA analysis facilities use VigyanLLM for human identification and genetic profiling applications that demand the highest specificity and reliability standards. Agricultural biotechnology researchers apply the platform to GMO testing, crop breeding marker development, and livestock genetics workflows, while microbiology and virology laboratories design universal and degenerate primers for pathogen surveillance, metagenomics, and microbiome profiling studies.

Why Sovereignty Matters

In an era where genomic data is among the most sensitive personal information that exists, data sovereignty is not optional — it is a fundamental ethical and legal requirement. VigyanLLM is architected from the ground up as a sovereign platform that never sends genomic sequences, clinical data, or proprietary research information to external cloud services or third-party APIs. Every inference, every alignment, and every validation check runs on the researcher's own infrastructure — on-premises or on dedicated hardware that the institution controls. This architecture eliminates the legal and ethical exposure of sending patient genomic sequences, clinical trial data, or proprietary compound structures to foreign servers, making VigyanLLM compatible with HIPAA, GDPR, and institutional data governance policies that prohibit external data transmission.

As an India-first company, VigyanLLM is committed to building sovereign AI infrastructure for the Global South. We price our plans in Indian rupees, process payments through Indian payment gateways, and design our platform to serve the needs of researchers in countries where enterprise biotech software licensing is economically inaccessible. Sovereignty at VigyanLLM means technological independence — a platform that does not depend on foreign AI APIs, does not require continuous internet connectivity for core functions, and places control of research data firmly in the hands of the institutions that generate it.
