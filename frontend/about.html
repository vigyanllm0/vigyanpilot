About VigyanLLM

We are building sovereign biomedical AI for India's research labs. No external APIs. No data leaving your infrastructure. Just validated science, delivered at the speed of thought.

Our Mission

VigyanLLM exists because Indian research labs deserve world-class bioinformatics tools that understand their constraints: limited budgets, strict data residency requirements, and the need for audit documentation that international granting bodies demand.

We believe that sovereign AI — AI that runs on your infrastructure, under your control, within your country's borders — is not a luxury. It is a necessity for institutions handling sensitive genomic and health data.

Our Technology

VigyanLLM is built on a proprietary multi-agent AI architecture with three coordinated agents:

  • Core Agent: Handles primary bioinformatics workflows including primer design, BLAST analysis, and thermodynamic calculations
  • SubBrain Agent: Performs deep analysis on specific sub-tasks like secondary structure prediction, SNP impact assessment, and multiplex compatibility scoring
  • ChinhAI (Verification Agent): Reviews all outputs for scientific accuracy, parameter compliance, and consistency before delivering results to the user

All processing runs through the VigyanInferenceEngine — our proprietary inference system that operates entirely on local infrastructure with zero external API calls.
