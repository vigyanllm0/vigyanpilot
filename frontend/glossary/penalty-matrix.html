penalty matrix

Primer Design Search volume: low Schema: DefinedTerm

Definition

A weighted scoring system used in VigyanLLM Step 19 to rank and prioritize primer candidates based on how well they pass all 18 preceding validation checks. Each check contributes a penalty score based on how close the primer is to passing or failing the threshold. The final penalty score aggregates all individual penalties to produce a single ranking metric for primer selection.
