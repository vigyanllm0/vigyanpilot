reverse primer

Primer Design Search volume: high Schema: DefinedTerm

Definition

The primer that anneals to the sense (positive) strand of DNA and extends in the opposite direction relative to the forward primer during PCR. Together with the forward primer, it defines both boundaries of the amplified region and determines the final amplicon size.

In Practice

reverse primer is widely used in primer design and related fields. Key applications include:
