Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-09

Data We Process

VigyanLLM may process account details, sequence inputs, accession queries, design parameters, generated reports, usage records, and payment metadata needed to operate the service.

Payment Data

Payments are processed by Razorpay. VigyanLLM stores payment identifiers and reconciliation metadata, but does not store card numbers, CVV, or net-banking credentials.

Research Data

Sequence and report data should be treated as confidential research material. Production deployments should provide project retention controls and deletion workflows for labs and institutions.

Security

The production service should run over HTTPS with secure cookies, server-side rate limits, access controls, and audit logging.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to contact@vigyanllm.in.
