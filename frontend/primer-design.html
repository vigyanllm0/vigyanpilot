What is this tool?

This automated primer design tool runs a 24-check validation pipeline including Primer3 design, BLAST specificity checking, SNP screening, repeat masking, TaqMan probe design, and multiplex scoring. It is designed for molecular biology researchers who need validated, audit-ready primer pairs.

Design validated primer pairs and TaqMan probes in under 30 seconds. VigyanLLM Primer runs a 24-step validation pipeline — thermodynamics, BLAST specificity, SNP screening, repeat masking, and multiplex scoring — and produces lab-ready results with no external API calls.

24-step Primer Design Validation Pipeline

Thermodynamic Profiling

SantaLucia 1998 nearest-neighbour thermodynamics for accurate Tm, delta-G, and hairpin/dimer predictions. Supports monovalent and divalent cation corrections.

  • Primer melting temperature (Tm)
  • Hairpin and self-dimer checks
  • Cross-dimer (heterodimer) analysis
  • Delta-G threshold filtering

BLAST Specificity

Primer-BLAST against nr, refseq_rna, refseq_genomic, and custom databases. Excludes non-target organisms with configurable taxonomy filters.

  • NCBI BLAST+ integration
  • Taxonomic filter (human, mouse, rat, virus)
  • Off-target mismatch threshold
  • 3' end specificity check

SNP & Repeat Screening

Filters primers overlapping known SNPs (dbSNP, COSMIC) and repetitive elements (RepeatMasker, simple repeats, low complexity regions).

  • dbSNP overlap detection
  • COSMIC mutation screening
  • RepeatMasker annotation
  • Simple repeat and homopolymer filters

TaqMan Probe Design

Dual-labelled hydrolysis probe design with Tm differential optimisation, GC content balancing, and dye-quencher compatibility validation.

  • Probe Tm 5-10°C above primer Tm
  • FAM, VIC, HEX, ROX, Cy5 dye support
  • BHQ and MGB quencher options
  • Probe secondary structure check

Multiplex PCR Design

Design primers for 2-plex to 6-plex assays with amplicon size differentiation, Tm harmonisation, and cross-reactivity elimination.

  • Amplicon sizing (60-500 bp)
  • Tm harmonisation (±1°C)
  • Cross-dimer matrix analysis
  • Primer concentration balancing

Audit-Ready Reports

Download complete design reports with full validation metrics, primer sequences, primer locations, and QC scores. Multiple export formats.

  • PDF audit report
  • CSV/JSON data export
  • IDT and Twist order-ready formats
  • Primer location maps

Frequently Asked Questions About Primer Design Tool

Everything you need to know about using this tool

Is the Primer Design tool free?

The primer design tool offers 5 free designs per user. Each design runs a multi-stage validation pipeline connecting Primer3, BLAST, and thermodynamics modules on our servers. After 5 free uses, a subscription is required.

What makes VigyanLLM primer design different?

VigyanLLM combines Primer3 thermodynamics with SantaLucia nearest-neighbour Tm calculations, BLAST specificity screening, and a 24-check validation pipeline to produce optimized primer pairs. This deterministic pipeline simultaneously evaluates Tm, GC content, specificity, secondary structure, and amplicon size.

What types of primers can I design?

You can design standard PCR primers, qPCR primers, sequencing primers, multiplex primers, degenerate primers, bisulfite PCR primers, and allele-specific primers.

How does specificity checking work?

Designed primers are automatically BLASTed against NCBI databases to verify target-specific amplification and detect potential off-target binding.

Do I need an account?

No, you can use primer design without an account for your first 5 designs. Creating an account unlocks usage tracking and subscription management.

What happens after 5 free uses?

You can subscribe to continue using the tool. Subscriptions start at ₹99 (~$1.20 USD) (~$1.19 USD)/day for the Daily Pass (5 primer designs + 2 docking runs) with monthly plans from ₹2,499 (~$30 USD)/month.
