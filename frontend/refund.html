Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-09

Top-Up Credits

Unused top-up credits may be reviewed for a refund on request. Credits consumed by completed design jobs are generally not refundable because compute and database checks have already been performed.

Subscriptions

Subscription refunds are reviewed case by case, especially for duplicate payments, failed checkout reconciliation, accidental purchase, or service unavailability.

Failed Payments

If Razorpay reports a successful payment but VigyanLLM credits are not added, contact support with the payment ID and order ID for reconciliation.

Contact

Refund requests can be sent to contact@vigyanllm.in.
