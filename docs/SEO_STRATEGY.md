## SECTION 5: FAQ & AEO CONTENT BLOCKS

### 5.1 Homepage FAQ (8 Questions)

**Q1: What is VigyanLLM Primer?**
A: VigyanLLM Primer is a research-use-only primer and probe design workflow that combines Primer3-style design with a 22-step validation pipeline covering sequence validation, thermodynamics, primer Tm, GC content, hairpin/self-dimer/cross-dimer risk, specificity/off-target review, repeat masking, SNP risk, amplicon structure, multiplex scoring, manufacturing feasibility, probe readiness, preferred-best sequence binding maps, and exportable assay reports.

**Q2: Is VigyanLLM a clinical diagnostic tool?**
A: No. VigyanLLM outputs are research-use-only (RUO) in silico recommendations and require independent wet-lab validation. VigyanLLM is not intended for clinical diagnostic use and does not store patient data.

**Q3: What makes VigyanLLM different from Primer3?**
A: Primer3 designs primers; VigyanLLM helps labs validate, rank, document, export, and order safer assay candidates. VigyanLLM adds reporting, specificity review, thermodynamic ranking, preferred-best sequence maps, and vendor-ready exports. Think of it as a workflow layer on top of Primer3-style design.

**Q4: Can I use VigyanLLM for CRISPR guide design?**
A: VigyanLLM Primer focuses on PCR primer and TaqMan probe design. The broader VigyanLLM platform (on-premise deployment) includes CRISPR guide design, molecular docking, and consensus workflows.

**Q5: Does VigyanLLM work offline?**
A: The public VigyanLLM Primer is web-based. For fully on-premise deployment with offline access, contact our team for enterprise licensing.

**Q6: What data does VigyanLLM store?**
A: VigyanLLM does not store user sequences, primer designs, or project data beyond the current session unless explicitly exported. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See our privacy policy for details.

**Q7: How much does VigyanLLM Primer cost?**
A: VigyanLLM Primer offers individual, institutional, and corporate licenses. See pricing page for current rates. Free 7-day trials are available for evaluation.

**Q8: Is VigyanLLM available for Indian labs?**
A: Yes. VigyanLLM is India-based and offers Razorpay payment integration, Indian pricing, and support for Indian academic institutions and biotech startups.

17. Probe Tm validation
18. Report generation (PDF)
19. CSV export
20. JSON export
21. Vendor-ready IDT format
22. Vendor-ready Twist format

**Q10: What does "preferred-best sequence map" mean?**
A: It is a per-run binding map showing the highest-ranked primer pair on your template, including forward and reverse primer binding positions, amplicon length, and highlighted template context. This helps you verify the design visually.

**Q11: Can VigyanLLM design multiplex assays?**
A: Yes. VigyanLLM includes multiplex compatibility checks, scoring, and optimized reports for multiplexed assays. It ranks primer pairs by multiplex feasibility.

**Q12: Does VigyanLLM support TaqMan probes?**
A: Yes. VigyanLLM includes TaqMan probe design, probe Tm validation, secondary structure checks, and vendor-ready TaqMan report formats.

**Q13: What file formats can I export?**
A: VigyanLLM exports primer and probe designs in PDF (report), CSV, JSON, IDT (for ordering), Twist (for ordering), and vendor-agnostic formats.

**Q14: Can VigyanLLM help with qPCR primer design?**
A: Yes. VigyanLLM is optimized for qPCR primers, TaqMan probes, and real-time PCR assay workflows.

**Q15: Is the VigyanLLM report audit-ready?**
A: Yes. VigyanLLM generates audit-ready assay reports with full methodology, primer properties, validation results, and regulatory-compliant documentation suitable for lab records and regulatory submissions.
